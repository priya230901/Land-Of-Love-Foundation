About Us

Land of Love Foundation is a registered non-profit organisation in 2021, founded by J.M. Balaji. The intention of the trust is to provide education, skill development, employment support, health (blood network), and food distribution drives in Tamil Nadu and Kerala. We are committed to serving only for the welfare of the people without any discrimination.
