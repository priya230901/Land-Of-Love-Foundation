About Us

Land of Love Foundation is a registered non-profit organisation, founded in 2021 by J.M. Balaji. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to uplift and empower communities in Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and beyond through education, health, and social support, without any discrimination.

Our Mission

We are driven by a deep commitment to serve the welfare of all people. Whether it's providing education, improving health through our blood donation network, or supporting the underprivileged with essential services, our goal is to create lasting positive change in the lives of those who need it most.

Our Services

1. Free Education & Skill Development

Free Spoken English Classes, Self-Study Kits, and Personality Development Classes for college students and working professionals. Offering Placement Guidance & Job Assistance, with tie-ups to companies and NGOs for skill development and placement support.

2. Blood Donation & Health Support

Blood donation drives for emergencies, surgeries, accidents, and dialysis needs across Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Bangalore. A database of over 1,100 donors from colleges and IT professionals to ensure timely assistance.

3. Food Distribution Drives

Organising food distribution on special occasions like birthdays, anniversaries, and memorials for the homeless, orphanages, old-age homes, and needy communities.

4. Environmental Impact

Eco-Friendly Club: More than 895+ tree saplings planted across Tamil Nadu on various occasions as part of our commitment to a greener, healthier planet.

5. Spreading Joy with Magic Shows

Free magic shows for children in orphanages, elders in old-age homes, and students in special schools, bringing joy and laughter to those who need it the most.
